About the Event

Demystifying Blockchain is a biennial event dedicated to exploring the latest advancements in blockchain technology and Web3. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to understand and leverage the potential of this transformative technology.

Through insightful talks, engaging workshops, and networking opportunities, we aim to:

  • Demystify complex blockchain concepts
  • Inspire innovation and creativity
  • Foster a vibrant community of blockchain enthusiasts
